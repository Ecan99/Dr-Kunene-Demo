About Our Practice

We've moved from Tzaneen Crossing to our new location opposite Van Velden Hospital.

Welcome to Dr. Kunene Dental Surgery, now conveniently located at 3rd Avenue, Ext 4, Siesta Place No. 2, Tzaneen. Our modern facility is equipped with the latest dental technology to provide exceptional care for patients of all ages.

With a focus on patient comfort and dental excellence, we offer comprehensive dental solutions that help you maintain optimal oral health and achieve the smile you've always wanted.

Our team of experienced dental professionals is committed to providing gentle, personalized care in a relaxing environment. We understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful for some, which is why we've created a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can feel at ease.

Our Services

We provide a wide range of dental services to meet all your oral health needs

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments that remove stains and discoloration for a brighter smile.

Fillings

Restore damaged teeth with durable, natural-looking dental fillings.

Cleaning

Thorough dental cleanings to remove plaque and maintain oral hygiene.

Extractions

Safe and gentle tooth extractions for both adults and children.

Dental Implants

Permanent solution for missing teeth that look and function like natural teeth.

Permanent Crowns

Custom-made crowns to restore the shape, size, and strength of damaged teeth.

Braces

Orthodontic treatments to align teeth and improve your smile.

Gold Tooth Removal

Safe removal of gold teeth and slimming wires for improved aesthetics.

Dentures

Custom-fitted dentures (false teeth) to restore your smile and function.
